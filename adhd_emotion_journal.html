ADHD Emotion Journal

Digital journal to reflect, track, and manage emotions for individuals with ADHD

Project Overview

ADHD Emotion Journal is a tool designed to help individuals with ADHD track their emotions, reflect on triggers, and find strategies for emotional support. It provides a simple, interactive format that encourages self-awareness and emotional regulation.

Purpose: The journal aims to help users identify emotional patterns, understand the causes behind specific emotions, and ultimately provide a way for them to manage their emotional well-being more effectively. The design focuses on simplicity and accessibility, allowing users to easily engage with their emotions without feeling overwhelmed.

Project Date: April 2025

Skills Showcase

This project showcases a blend of creative design, technical skills, and emotional intelligence in the development of an AI-powered self-help tool. Here are some of the key skills used in building the ADHD Emotion Journal:

  • Prompt Engineering: Developed a series of dynamic, personalized prompts to guide users through emotional reflection.
  • No-Code AI Integration: Integrated AI tools into the journal, enabling data-driven insights into user emotions.
  • UX/UI Design: Designed an intuitive interface with a focus on accessibility and user experience.
  • Automation & Workflows: Automated parts of the journaling process to save users time and make the process more engaging.
  • Content Generation: Developed relevant and supportive content through AI for better emotional tracking and management.
  • Web Development (HTML/CSS): Built the platform using HTML and CSS for easy access on the web, making the app universally accessible.
